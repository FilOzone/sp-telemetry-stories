### Revised Actionable Retrieval Failure Categorization

#### Summary:
This document outlines the revised approach to categorizing actionable retrieval failures, focusing on compactness and clarity.  

#### Categories:
1. **Timeouts**: Situations where the system fails to respond within expected time limits.
2. **Network Errors**: Issues arising from connectivity problems that prevent data retrieval.
3. **Data Corruption**: Instances where retrieved data is incomplete or incorrectly formatted.
4. **Authentication Failures**: Occurrences when access is denied due to invalid credentials.
5. **Resource Unavailable**: Cases where the requested resource is not available for retrieval.
